Remote Water Cannon CROW - 40/65

A long-range wireless water-discharge solution for high-risk fire scenes

Our smart remote water cannon is a motorized water-discharge system designed to control repetitive and hazardous discharge operations through wireless remote operation.

01
Remote Drive Control - Remote left/right and up/down control via motorized PAN/TILT drive
- Intuitive operation from a safe distance using a wireless control pad
- Enhanced on-site responsiveness with a wide range of motion
PAN (left/right): approx. ±170° (340° total) / TILT (up/down): 0° ~ 90°
02
Convenient Oscillation Function - Automatically cycles within a set range, reducing the burden of discharge operations
- Forms a containment line across wide-area fire-spread zones
03
Communication & Safety Control Logic - Oscillation continues even if communication is lost during operation
- Safety mechanism activates if the equipment becomes unstable during discharge
- Prevents secondary accidents by halting control in emergency situations
04
40A / 65A Lineup - 40A: up to 1,000 LPM discharge performance
- 65A: up to 2,000 LPM discharge performance
- Available in vehicle-mounted or mobile configurations
